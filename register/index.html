The ideas and information shared at BIO go beyond professional development. You’ll experience powerful business partnering, benefit from invaluable education sessions and have the chance to network with 15,000 of the industry’s best and brightest.

Premium / Co-Located Events

Add these to your registration for an additional cost:

  • One-on-One PartneringRegister for Convention Access & Partnering to access more than 29,000 meetings in the BIO Business Forum and the BIO Exhibition. Partnering opens in April 2015.
  • Become a Company Presenter

    Submissions for Company Presentations opens in 2015! Company Presentations are held adjacent to the BIO Business Forum. If accepted, presenters will be required to register for Convention Access & Partnering in addition to paying a presentation fee. 

    Member Presentation: $1,200
    Non-Member Presentation: $1,500

  • BIO Entrepreneurship Bootcamp

    Join us for an intensive, two-day program that walks you through the necessary steps and gives you the skills to transform technology and invention into a viable company.  

    Add to your registration for $299. 

  • BIO Career FairThursday, June 18, 2:00 - 7:00pm in Hall G of the Pennsylvania Convention Center. The annual BIO Career Fair is an extension of BIO’s online job board—Bio Jobs—the Career Fair will provide an opportunity for meeting participants and top employers to discuss career opportunities. 

Additional Events

These events require separate registrations.

  • Executive Training ProgramsThe Business Development Fundamentals and Advanced Business Development courses train individuals who are making the deals that drive this industry. Each course is taught by experienced business development professionals from leading pharmaceutical and biotechnology firms, and experts from the fields of law and public relations. Email convention@bio.org with questions.
  • BioParks 2015 Discover how accelerating changes in big pharma's approach to drug development offers challenges and opportunities for university research enhancement and park growth. Locations and agenda will be announced soon!
  • Biotech Primer The Science Driving Biotech is a one-day, fast-paced course starting with an overview of scientific concepts required for understanding biotechnology applications. Registration opens in January. 
  • Community College Program  The 12th Annual Community College Program brings together community college faculty, high school teachers, industy and organizations focused on biotechnology to learn from each one and to develop a network of educational programs to support industry needs for biotechnicians.
